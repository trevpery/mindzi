ELIGIBILITY

Mindzi is open to students in Classes 6 to 9 enrolled in any recognised school in India. There is no restriction by school board - CBSE, ICSE, IB, Cambridge, and State Board students are all eligible.

Students must be registered by a parent or legal guardian. Self-registration by students is not permitted. By registering a child, the parent or guardian confirms they have the authority to do so and consent to the child's participation under these terms.

02

REGISTRATION AND PAYMENT

Pre-registration is free and places no financial obligation on you. It allows you to express interest in the Mindzi Future-Ready Challenge and receive updates as registrations open.

When registrations open in July 2026, a registration fee will be payable to complete enrolment for the School Round.

₹649

03

REFUND POLICY

Pre-registration is free and does not require any payment. As no fee is charged at the pre-registration stage, no refund applies.

Once the registration fee has been paid:

  • If the student has not yet accessed the Learning Platform, a full refund will be issued upon request within 7 days of payment.
  • If the student has accessed the Learning Platform, no refund will be issued, as access to the primary digital product and learning content has already been provided.
  • If Mindzi cancels the School Round or the competition for any reason, a full refund of the applicable registration fee will be issued to all registered students.

Refund requests must be sent to hello@mindziai.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and must include the student's name, school name, and registered email address.

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 working days.

Competition Format

Mindzi reserves the right to modify competition dates, schedules, round formats, question formats, qualification criteria, or operational procedures where reasonably necessary. Any material changes will be communicated through official Mindzi channels.

Results

Results are generally published within a reasonable period after the relevant exam window closes. While Mindzi takes reasonable care in processing scores and rankings, Mindzi reserves the right to correct any technical, administrative, or scoring errors identified after publication.

Rankings

Rankings are determined by the scoring methodology applicable to the relevant competition round. Where two or more students achieve the same score, tie-break criteria may be applied. For the 2026-27 edition of the Mindzi Future-Ready Challenge, the higher Signature Challenge score will determine the higher rank. If a tie remains unresolved, students may share the same rank.

All decisions relating to disqualification are final.

CERTIFICATES

Participants who complete a competition round are eligible to receive a digital certificate for that round. Certificates are generally issued within a reasonable period after results for the relevant round are published.

Depending on the round completed, a certificate may include:

  • The participant's rank within that round
  • The participant's percentile within that round
  • Scores across the assessed dimensions
  • The participant's strongest dimension or area of demonstrated strength

Mindzi may revise certificate formats, designs, and recognition categories from time to time.

Certificates are issued in digital format only. Participants are free to download, save, and print their certificates for personal use.

Participants are responsible for ensuring that registration details, including names, are entered accurately. Requests for corrections should be sent to hello@mindziai.com as soon as possible. While Mindzi will make reasonable efforts to accommodate correction requests before certificate generation, corrections cannot be guaranteed once a certificate has been issued.
